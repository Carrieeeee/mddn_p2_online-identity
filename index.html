Online Identity

Online identity, is the social identity that Internet users create online communities and websites. It can also be considered as a positive creation of self identity.

Online Communication

Compared with face-to-face communication, online communication can not express the true feelings of human beings, such as facial expressions, the tone of chat, and subtle details, which are blocked by the "network”.

Language

On our personal network, we usually use more relaxed language and rely on humour to convey messgaes. Further more, we will use some informal languages, such as short language to express.

Links

On the professional sense, we will post links that refer to our currently surveys or related academic knowledge. However, in personal spaces, we are more likely to post funny stories links or a general interesting article that our friends are interested in.

Images

Our professional self might be post a graph, whereas, our personal self is likely contains the photos of our families, our pets even our own lives.

Virtual Personality

Virtual personality refers to the deliberate or unintentional formation of a virtual image in a virtual community in the virtual world of the network, forming a certain virtual community status, and obtaining a certain virtual community evaluation.

MMORPG（Massively multiplayer online role-playing games）

In all role-playing games, players have to play a fictional role and control that role, complete the tasks, socialize, participate in certain activities.
